<p>    CHARLOTTE, N.C.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--
<a href="https://www.sonicautomotive.com/" rel="nofollow" shape="rect">Sonic Automotive, Inc.</a> (“Sonic Automotive” or “Sonic” or the “Company”) (<a href="https://ir.sonicautomotive.com/stock-data/quote" rel="nofollow" shape="rect">NYSE:SAH</a>), one of the nation’s largest automotive retailers, today announced it will release fiscal 2026 first quarter financial results on Thursday, April 30, 2026 by 7:00 A.M. (Eastern). Senior management will hold a conference call later that morning at 11:00 A.M. (Eastern).

</p>
<p>
Investor presentation and earnings press release materials will be accessible beginning the morning of the conference call on the Company’s website at <a href="https://ir.sonicautomotive.com" rel="nofollow" shape="rect">ir.sonicautomotive.com</a>.

</p>
<p>
To access the live webcast of the conference call, please go to <a href="https://ir.sonicautomotive.com" rel="nofollow" shape="rect">ir.sonicautomotive.com</a> and select the webcast link at the top of the page.

</p>
<p>
To dial in to the conference call via telephone, please dial (877) 407-8289 (domestic) or +1 (201) 689-8341 (international) and ask to be connected to the Sonic Automotive First Quarter 2026 Earnings Conference Call.

</p>
<p>
Dial-in access remains available throughout the live call, however, to ensure you are connected for the full call we suggest dialing in at least 10 minutes before the start of the call. A webcast replay will be available following the call for 14 days at <a href="https://ir.sonicautomotive.com" rel="nofollow" shape="rect">ir.sonicautomotive.com</a>.

</p>

<b><span class="bwuline">About Sonic Automotive</span></b>

<p>
Sonic Automotive, Inc., a Fortune 500 company based in Charlotte, North Carolina, is on a quest to become the most valuable diversified automotive retail and service brand in America. Our Company culture thrives on creating, innovating, and providing industry-leading guest experiences, driven by strategic investments in technology, teammates, and ideas that ultimately fulfill ownership dreams, enrich lives, and deliver happiness to our guests and teammates. As one of the largest automotive and powersports retailers in America, we are committed to delivering on this goal while pursuing expansive growth and taking progressive measures to be the leader in these categories. Our new platforms, programs, and people are set to drive the next generation of automotive and powersports experiences.
